Privacy Policy


Last Updated 11/02/2023

Welcome to DjangoStream. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to safeguard your data.

1. Information Collected

Account Information:  When you create an account with us, we collect your name, email address, and password.

Payment Information: To process transactions, we may collect payment details, but we do not store full credit card numbers or security codes.

Usage Data: We gather data on how you interact with our platform, such as watch history, search queries, and preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve our services.

Communication: To communicate with you about updates, promotions, and customer service inquiries.

Security: To detect fraud, protect against malicious activity, and ensure the integrity of our services.

3. Protection of Your Data

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion.

We use encryption technologies to ensure the confidentiality of your information.

4. Sharing of Information

No Sale of Data:  We firmly commit that we will not sell your personal information to any third party.

Service Providers:  We may share your data with third-party vendors who provide services on our behalf, such as payment processing. These vendors are contractually bound to protect your information.

Legal Obligations:  We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to legal processes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your personal information at any time.

You can opt-out of promotional communications by following the unsubscribe link in our emails.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your user experience, remember your preferences, and provide relevant advertising. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes and encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at help@django-stream.stream.

Thank you for choosing DjangoStream. We are dedicated to ensuring a secure and enjoyable streaming experience for all our users.
